Future Vision

Established in 1987 KT is a fabric supplier and converter servicing the apparel and home furnishing markets. We specialize in Japanese fabric : Cotton, Rayon, Linen, and other natural fiber/fiber blends in various constructions. We also offer extensive knit, yarn dyed, print, novelty, synthetic, and active-wear programs. In an effort to move into a more sustainable direction, our selection of Eco-friendly fabrics continues to grow with options available across the sustainable spectrum.
